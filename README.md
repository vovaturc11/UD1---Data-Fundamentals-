## Topic

Student Survival Budget Calculator

## What It Does

This program estimates a monthly student budget. It uses financial aid, work hours, hourly pay, and common living expenses to calculate total income, total expenses, percent of income spent, and money left after expenses.

This topic is useful for community college students because many students have to balance school, work, rent, food, transportation, and school supplies. A simple budget estimate can help a student see if their monthly plan is realistic while they stay focused on classes.
